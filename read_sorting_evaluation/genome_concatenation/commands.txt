To find out if the mapping position is correct in a bam file from concatenated genomes:

samtools view file.bam | cut -f 3 | cut -d "_" -f 2 | awk '$1>2239{c++} END{print c+0}'

To perform alignment:

$data=/srv/kenlab/stefan/phdProject/bs_data_analysis/00_data
$genome=/srv/kenlab/stefan/phdProject/Akam_genome/
$temp=/srv/kenlab/stefan/phdProject/eagle_test/
